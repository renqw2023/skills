# Intimate Wellbeing Checklist (non‑explicit)

## Quick triage
- Duration of issue (days/weeks/months)
- Pain, bleeding, fever, or sudden change? → medical consult
- Medication changes or stress triggers?

## Habits & lifestyle
- Sleep 7–9h
- Exercise 3–4x/week
- Reduce alcohol/smoking
- Manage stress (breathing, routine)

## Relationship & communication
- Discuss expectations
- Slow down, reduce pressure
- Align on timing and comfort
