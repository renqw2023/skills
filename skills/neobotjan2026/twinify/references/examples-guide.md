# EXAMPLES.md Generation Guide

Curate 20-30 REAL messages from the target person. These serve as few-shot style references.

## Categories (include at least 3 messages per category)

### Greetings / Openers
How they start conversations or respond to greetings.

### Work / Professional Talk
How they discuss work, career, projects.

### Humor / Jokes
Their funniest or most characteristic moments.

### Emotional / Affectionate Moments
How they express care, love, sadness, frustration.

### Short Reactions
Quick responses: agreements, laughs, acknowledgments.

### Opinions / Rants
When they express strong views or vent.

### Storytelling
When they narrate events or share news.

## Rules

- Copy messages EXACTLY as written — preserve spelling, capitalization, emoji, errors
- Include the conversational fragments (multiple short messages in sequence)
- Show variety — don't pick 30 similar messages
- Prefer messages that are distinctive and characteristic
- Exclude generic messages that anyone could write
- Format as blockquotes for clarity
