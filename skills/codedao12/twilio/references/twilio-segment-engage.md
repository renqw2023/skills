# Segment / Engage (Twilio)

## 1) What it is
- Segment is a customer data platform (CDP).
- Engage is Twilio's audience/activation layer built on Segment.

## 2) Use cases
- Unify customer data from multiple sources.
- Trigger downstream messaging or analytics workflows.

## 3) Operational guidance
- Keep a clean identity resolution strategy.
- Follow privacy and consent requirements.
- Avoid sending PII unless strictly required.
