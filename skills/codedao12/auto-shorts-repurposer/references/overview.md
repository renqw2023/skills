# Overview

## Scope

- Turn long-form media into short-form clip plans.
- Emphasize hooks, clarity, and pacing over raw highlights.

## Workflow

- Collect source media and transcript.
- Segment into candidate moments.
- Score moments by hook strength, clarity, and replay value.
- Select clips and draft packaging (titles, captions, hashtags).
- Produce a cut list with timestamps and notes.

## Quality bar

- Hook appears within the first 1 to 2 seconds.
- Clip contains a single clear idea.
- Remove dead air and low energy sections.
- Captions are readable and match speech timing.

## Output bundle

- Clip list with timestamp ranges and hooks.
- Captions and on-screen text drafts.
- Title and hashtag options.
- Thumbnail or cover-frame notes.
